About Hardik Pandya

Hardik Himanshu Pandya (born 11 October 1993) is an Indian international cricketer and the captain of the Mumbai Indians in the IPL. An all-rounder who bowls right-arm fast-medium deliveries and bats right-handed in the middle-order, Pandya has represented India in all 3 formats. He occasionally plays for his regional team Baroda in domestic cricket. He was the vice captain of the Indian team that won the 2024 T20 World Cup.
